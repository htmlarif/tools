FAQ

How to compress image to 100KB?
Quality 55–70, WebP format, and optionally resize width/height. Then compress and check the output size.
Does it upload my photos?
No. Everything runs inside your browser—no server upload.
PNG vs JPG vs WebP কোনটা ভালো?
Photos-এর জন্য JPG/WebP ভালো। WebP সাধারণত ছোট সাইজ দেয়। Logo/transparent দরকার হলে PNG ব্যবহার করুন।
Can I compress multiple images at once?
Yes. Add multiple images and download all results as a ZIP.
Will compression reduce quality?
Lower quality means smaller size but may reduce clarity. Use Medium/High presets for balanced results.
